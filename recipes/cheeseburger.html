Cheeseburger Recipe

Classic Cheeseburger (X-Burger)

This classic cheeseburger recipe is a perfect combination of juicy beef, melted cheese, and your favorite toppings.


Cheeseburger Ingredients

Here's what you'll need to make a classic cheeseburger:

  • Ground beef: High-quality ground beef for a juicy patty.

  • Buns: Fresh burger buns to hold your delicious creation.

  • Cheese: Your choice of cheese slices (cheddar, American, Swiss, etc.)

  • Lettuce: Fresh lettuce leaves for crunch.

  • Tomato: Sliced ripe tomatoes.

  • Onion: Thinly sliced onions for a savory kick.

  • Pickles: Optional, but they add a nice tangy flavor.

  • Ketchup and Mustard: Classic condiments for extra flavor.

  • Salt and Pepper: To season the beef patty.

Ingredients Measurements

  • 1 pound ground beef

  • 4 burger buns

  • 4 slices of cheese

  • Lettuce leaves

  • 1 large tomato, sliced

  • 1 onion, thinly sliced

  • Pickles (optional)

  • Ketchup and Mustard

  • Salt and Pepper to taste

How to Make Cheeseburgers

Follow these steps to create a classic cheeseburger:

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.

  2. Season each patty with salt and pepper.

  3. Cook the patties on a grill or stovetop over medium-high heat until they reach your desired level of doneness.

  4. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.

  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.

  6. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on the bottom half of each bun.

  7. Top with lettuce, tomato slices, onions, pickles (if using), and condiments.

  8. Cover with the top half of the bun and press gently to secure everything.

  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious cheeseburgers!
