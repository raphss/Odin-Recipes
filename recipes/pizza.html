Classic Margherita Pizza

This classic Margherita pizza recipe is simple and delicious, letting the flavors of the fresh ingredients shine.


Pizza Ingredients

Here's a list of ingredients you'll need to make a classic Margherita pizza:

  • Dough: You can use store-bought pizza dough or make your own.

  • Tomatoes: Fresh, ripe tomatoes or cherry tomatoes for the sauce.

  • Mozzarella cheese: High-quality mozzarella for that gooey, melty goodness.

  • Basil: Fresh basil leaves for a burst of flavor.

  • Olive oil: For drizzling over the pizza before baking.

  • Salt and pepper: To taste.

Ingredients Measurements

  • 1 pound pizza dough

  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes, thinly sliced

  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced

  • Handful of fresh basil leaves

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to Make Pizza

Follow these steps to create a delicious Margherita pizza:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.

  4. Spread the tomato slices evenly over the dough.

  5. Arrange the mozzarella slices on top of the tomatoes.

  6. Scatter fresh basil leaves over the pizza.

  7. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and season with salt and pepper.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.

  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy!
